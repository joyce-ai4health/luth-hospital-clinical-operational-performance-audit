# LUTH Hospital Clinical & Operational Performance Audit

## Project Overview

Healthcare analytics project focused on evaluating hospital operations, patient flow, billing efficiency, departmental performance, and length of stay (LOS) patterns using the LUTH Hospital Enhanced Clinical & Operations Dataset.

The project leverages Excel, SQL, Python, and Power BI to identify operational bottlenecks and generate data-driven recommendations for improving healthcare service delivery and resource utilization.

---

## Business Problem

Hospitals generate large volumes of operational and clinical data daily. However, without proper analysis, inefficiencies in patient flow, billing processes, resource allocation, and departmental workload distribution may remain hidden.

This project aims to analyze operational and clinical performance metrics within Lagos University Teaching Hospital (LUTH) and provide actionable insights that can support healthcare administrators in improving service delivery, resource management, and operational efficiency.

---

## Dataset Information

### Dataset Name

LUTH Hospital Enhanced Clinical & Operations Data

### Source

Kaggle

### Dataset Description

This dataset captures operational and clinical performance data from Lagos University Teaching Hospital (LUTH), one of Nigeria's major tertiary healthcare institutions.

The dataset includes patient admissions, discharge records, billing information, treatment details, staff activities, and hospital operational metrics.

### Key Features

* Patient admissions and discharge records
* Departmental workload analysis
* Diagnosis and treatment information
* Billing and surgery cost metrics
* Laboratory test and medication records
* Ward allocation and patient flow tracking
* Length of Stay (LOS) analysis
* Doctor and departmental activity monitoring

---

## Project Objectives

* Analyze patient admission and discharge patterns
* Evaluate departmental workload and performance
* Assess billing and healthcare cost trends
* Investigate factors affecting Length of Stay (LOS)
* Monitor patient flow and ward utilization
* Identify operational bottlenecks
* Generate data-driven recommendations for hospital management
* Develop interactive healthcare dashboards

---

## Key Business Questions

1. Which departments handle the highest patient volumes?
2. What factors contribute to longer hospital stays?
3. Which departments generate the highest billing revenue?
4. How does patient flow vary across wards and departments?
5. Are there opportunities to improve operational efficiency?
6. What trends exist in treatments, laboratory testing, and medication administration?
7. How can hospital resources be allocated more effectively?
